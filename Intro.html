About Us

Viovox Entertainment Pvt Ltd is one of its kind Travel entertainment startup which provides in-flight like entertainment to the passengs in long route busses. We use Bring your device model to provide entertainment on the go even if the internet connectivity is low or zero! We are based out in pune and currently partnered with various bus operators accross gujrat and maharastra.

Our Product

We provide an WLAN based offline entertainment system which can be used in long route journeys when the internet is fluctuating and the passengers have nothing to do. Once the system is installed in Bus, it can be accessed using our application which is available on app store and apple store. Once logged in, Passengers can stream online content ranging from different genere' without any buffering and streaming.
